Proven strategies to acing any exam

These strategies apply only if you have a week before the exam.

1. Find a Youtube Tutorial for your course. For example if you are studying European History

During the Exam:

Label each question, EG (Educated Guess), CG (Complete Guess), or answer it if you know. Get through all the questions quickly, then move forward!

After the Exam:

I usually find it very helpful to chat with others after the exam. This will help relieve stress as if both of you get the same answers then your chances of doing well are high!
